FakeNewsDetector

Explore how Machine Learning can be leveraged to combat Fake News..

This product demonstrates the use of different Artificial Intelligence technologies, purely Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing to alleviate the problem of Fake News in social media by determining if a story is real or hoax.

This may not be completeley reliable per se as checking the veracity of a news story is a complex and cumbersome procedure, even for trained experts. So, with the assistance of a real-life human fact checking,human judgement and some reasoning skills, this product would yeild remarkable results.
